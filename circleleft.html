Circle Left

Details

Circle Left is a gender neutral contra dance in Oakland on the first Saturday of the month. We use larks and robins as our role names. Click here for our calendar.

Lake Merritt Dance Center
200 Grand Avenue
Hall 1, upstairs

Introductory lesson: 6:30 - 7 p.m.
Dance: 7 - 10 p.m.

$5-20 sliding scale
Or pay what you can. Everyone's welcome!
Cash preferred. We also take Venmo.

Drive: free parking (parking lot and street)
Transit: 1/2 mile from the 19th St. BART Station; or use AC Transit buses 12 or 33

Health and Safety

Masks are optional at Circle Left. We support and welcome dancers to continue to mask, and ask that you stay home if you're sick. The hall has air filters and some ventilation.
