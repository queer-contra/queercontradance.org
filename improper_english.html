Improper English

Improper English is a gender neutral English country dance in Berkeley on the second Tuesday of each month. Click here for our calendar.

Berkeley Fellowship of Unitarian Universalists, Fellowship Hall
1924 Cedar St (@ Bonita), Berkeley

Introductory lesson: 6:50 - 7 p.m.
Dance: 7 - 9:30 p.m.

$5-25 sliding scale. No one will be turned away for lack of funds, and all payment amounts are confidential. We accept cash or Venmo.

  • Drive: free street parking
  • Transit: 10-20 minute walk from Downtown Berkeley BART Station or North Berkeley BART Station; or use AC Transit bus 79

    • Code of Conduct

    We want all people to feel safe and comfortable at Queer Contra Dance. We value and expect consent and communication, community mindness, and physical safety. Harassment of any kind won't be tolerated. If someone's behavior is offensive or harassing during the dance, we encourage you to speak with one of the organizers.

    Please read our Values and Expectations if you would like to know more about our community norms and how we handle unwelcome behavior.

    Health and Safety

    Improper English is a masked dance. KN95+ masks are required at Improper English, and we ask that you stay home if you're sick.
    For more information, email: english@queercontradance.org
